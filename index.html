About Me

After 10 years in the Audio/Visual Industry, I decided it was time for a change. During my 10 years at Blue Ridge A/V and Lighting, the time in which I had to troubleshoot and solve problems was always my favorite. I decided to search for a way in which I could do that for a living. And that is when it happened...

I found General Assembly. A place to help me learn how to code. A community that would help me get to problem solve for a living. After pushing through the intense course, I am now better prepared to be a freelance Web Developer.

So here I am. A Freelance Web Developer who loves coding, playing video games (like this one I made), playing Cards Agains Humanity, and getting show tunes stuck in my head just so I can try to get them out again.
